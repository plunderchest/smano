About Me...

Hi, I'm Sharaz!

I have a passion for innovative design and enjoyable user experiences, with a particular focus on responsive web design. I feel most comfortable at the convergence of design and development.

Over my career, I have been involved in crafting solutions for ThomasNet - where I helped design the online presence of over 200 industrial companies, RealeFlow - where I helped design and prototype online tools for use by real estate investors, and Deloitte - where I currently lead the UI design for a suite of internal and client facing market intelligence applications, while being mindful of branding, best practices and the evolving web landscape.

I like simplicity, learning new things, optimizing, and am an avid do-it-yourself-er. I have an addiction to toying with new tech gadgets, and enjoy exploring different countries and cultures.

Random skill: I can solve a Rubik's cube in under 2 minutes!
